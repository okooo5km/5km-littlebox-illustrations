## Forward-Test Case: Workflow

Input idea:

```text
Raw meeting notes become three outputs: decision log, customer follow-up, and internal task list.
```

Expected direction: Closed Littlebox gathers messy notes with side-seam twig arms and folds them into three small sealed bundles placed outside the box. Avoid a straight pipeline diagram. The lid stays closed.

## Forward-Test Case: Overload

Input idea:

```text
An AI system fails when context keeps accumulating but never gets packaged into decisions.
```

Expected direction: Closed Littlebox braces against an external overflowing context pile; coral marks the jam; one clean sealed package is missing or unfinished. The lid stays closed.

## Forward-Test Case: Trust

Input idea:

```text
Trust grows when small pieces of evidence are preserved and handed over at the right moment.
```

Expected direction: Closed Littlebox gathers small evidence slips with side-seam twig arms and stamps one sealed package. Use external slips and packages to show the transfer.
